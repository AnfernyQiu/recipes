Ingredients:
-Glutinous rice flour
-fine sugar
-sesame
-lard
-egg
-water

Directions:
First make glutinous rice stuffing. Water, granulated sugar, lard into the pot together

Then make water oil skin, put flour, sugar, whole egg, lard, water, all mixed, knead smooth soft dough (depending on the flour water absorption, the amount of water required to adjust as appropriate, must be bunched up into a soft dough, not too dry)

Then make pastry. Mix the flour and lard and continue kneading until into the group.
